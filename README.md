# Customer-Purchasing-Behavior_-Analysis-Excel-Dashboard-
An interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze customer shopping behavior, purchasing trends, and sales performance using real-world retail data. This project transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through dynamic visualizations, KPIs, pivot tables, slicers, and charts.

# Key Objectives
Analyze customer purchasing patterns
Identify top-performing product categories
Track seasonal sales trends
Understand customer demographics and behavior
Evaluate discount and subscription impact on sales
Monitor customer satisfaction through review ratings

# Dashboard Features
  KPI Metrics
  -Total Revenue
  -Total Customers
  -Average Purchase Amount
  -Average Review Rating
  -Repeat Customers
  -Total Orders
  
# Visualizations Included
  Sales by Category
   -Analyzes revenue contribution across different product categories.
  Gender-wise Sales Analysis
   -Compares purchasing behavior between male and female customers.
  Season-wise Sales Trend
   -Identifies peak shopping seasons and seasonal demand patterns.
  Location-wise Revenue
   -Shows top-performing locations based on sales revenue.
  Review Rating Analysis
   -Evaluates customer satisfaction distribution through ratings.
  Subscription Status Analysis
   -Tracks subscribed vs non-subscribed customer behavior.
  Payment Method Analysis
   -Identifies the most preferred payment methods.
  Shipping Type Analysis
   -Compares customer preferences for shipping methods.
  Discount Impact Analysis
   -Measures how discounts influence customer spending.
  Frequency of Purchases
   -Analyzes customer repeat purchasing behavior.
